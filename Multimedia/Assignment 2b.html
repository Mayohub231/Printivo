Poetry

Definition of Poetry

Poetry is a form of literary art that uses aesthetic and often rhythmic qualities of language to evoke meanings
in addition to,or in place of,literal or surface level meanings.The instance of poetry is called a poem and is
written by a poet.Poems consists of lines,stanzas and verses.It makes use of different literal devices such as
assonance,alliteration,euphony,onomatopoeia and rhythms.Poetry is a form art used to express emotions or
thoughts towards a topic.

History of Poetry

The earliest poetry is believed to have been recited or sung employed as a way of remembering oral history,law and genealogy.Many scholars
particularly those researching the Homeric Traditioj and the oral epics of the of the Balkans,suggest that early writing shows clear
traces of older oral tradtions,including the use of repeated phrases as building blocks in larger poetic units.
